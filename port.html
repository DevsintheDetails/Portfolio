About Me

My interest in software development began in my teens when I just happened to be in the offices of the company I worked for and got to see how the company created their website. I was amazed at just how much one could accomplish with the right knowledge of computers. I was a professional skateboarder at the time and the team had little input into how the site would ultimately turn out, but the developer asked each of us a few questions about our interest and musical tastes. When the developer had finished we were blown away with what he had done. So a few of the reasons software development drew my interest are:

  • Building websites that immediately catch the eye.
  • Working with a dedicated group to solve problems
  • Constanly learning and evolving my craft.
